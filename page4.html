Source: http://vietnamnet.vn

Along with My Thuan Bridge, Can Tho Bridge significantly contributes in promoting Can Tho tourism in particular and Mekong Delta in general. The bridge is the point of connection between past and future opening a new phase in the development of the city.
Can Tho Bridge is a cable-stayed bridge with the South East Asia's longest main span (main span is 550 meters in length). Not only owning important significance in terms of economy, Can Tho Bridge is also the fascinating tourist site in Can Tho travel significantly contributing in the development of the city.

Data of Can Tho Bridge

Source: https://www.alotrip.com/

  • The tour total bridge is 15.85 miles, being the size of the main bridge 2.75 miles.
  • Can Tho bridge width is 26 meters, which translates to 4 lanes and 2 wide verges (initially intended for pedestrians, but in reality are taken over by motorcycle).
  • The highest point of the bridge is 171 meters in height.
  • The bridge has been suspended by 1010 kilometers of cable.
  • The Can Tho bridge construction work started on 25 September 2004 and ended on April 12, 2010.
  • The total cost of the project was 342 million dollars, what becomes the most expensive of Vietnam bridge to the Can Tho bridge. The bridge was funded with the help of Japan, which contributed 85% of the cost of the bridge.
